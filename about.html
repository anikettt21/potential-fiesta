About Us

ResumeForge was built with a simple mission: Make professional resume creation accessible to everyone.

Our Story

We saw that too many job seekers struggle with confusing word processors, uncooperative formatting, and expensive online services. Creating a well-designed, ATS-friendly resume shouldn't require a design degree or a monthly subscription. So we built ResumeForge—an entirely free, browser-based solution.

Why ResumeForge?

  • Privacy First: Your resume data is exclusively processed in your browser. We don't have servers harvesting your personal experience.
  • 100% Free: No hidden fees, no premium subscriptions, and no paywalls when you go to download your PDF.
  • Professional Designs: Choose from over 58 curated styles aimed at getting you past Applicant Tracking Systems and impressing human recruiters.

Commitment to Excellence

We are constantly refining our builder, adding new templates, and improving overall performance to ensure you land the interview for your dream job.
