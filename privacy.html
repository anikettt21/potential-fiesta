Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2024

At ResumeForge, your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we receive and collect when you use our website, and how we safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

ResumeForge does not collect, store, or transmit your resume data to any external servers. All data you enter into the builder is saved locally on your device or discarded when you leave the page. We do not require you to create an account, thereby minimizing any personal data collection.

2. Local Storage

We may use your browser's Local Storage to temporarily save your resume progress or application preferences. This data remains on your personal device and is not accessible by us or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use standard analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to help understand how users interact with our site. These tools do not collect personal identifying information beyond basic usage data and IP addresses.

4. Your Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

5. Changes to our Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page. We encourage you to review it regularly.

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us.
